Who we are

We take sites from first look to energized.

EV Edison is a developer, owner, and operator of high-power fleet charging for Class 4–8 electric trucks, and a leading advisor on fleet electrification. We handle site scoping, utility interconnection, permitting, construction, and 24/7 operations — and where the grid isn't ready yet, we roll in mobile power from Power Edison.

What we do

Secure park & charge hubs

Gated, monitored yards where Class 4–8 trucks park and charge on the same footprint — day and overnight.

DC fast charging

Dispensers sized for light, medium, and heavy-duty duty cycles, from depot-overnight to high-power turnarounds.

Site scoping & development

Feasibility, utility interconnection, permitting, and incentive capture — the work that decides whether a site pencils.

Installation & maintenance

In-house engineering and construction crews for EV Edison sites and third-party charging sites alike.

Mobile charging hubs

Power Edison mobile batteries deliver charging almost anywhere — including sites still waiting on a service.

Fleet electrification consulting

Route and duty-cycle analysis, rate design, and infrastructure planning for fleets going electric.
